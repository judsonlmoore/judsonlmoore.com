Pursuing happiness

What does it take to be happy? No one can tell you the answer; you must discover it for yourself!

Nothing is more personal than defining what "happiness" means to you, but it is a complicated topic. Check out these articles about pursuing happiness where I share my thoughts on the topic.

Community engagement

My roots from an activist family, origin-story involving Rotary International, and years of volunteerism around the world have all contributed to my commitment to community.

I believe that having a strong commitment to community is enriching for everyone involved.

Some of my favorite community organizations include Rotary and the Red Cross.

Empowering youth

After years of travel blogging and being asked how I've lived such an incredible life, I realized that it is time to pay forward the lessons and encouragements instilled in my at a young age.

In my book, Exponential Happiness, I reveal all the words of wisdom that nudged me out the door when I was young. In addition, I share many of the professional tools that have helped me stay on-track with my goals.

These topics are also shared on the blog for happiness and lifestyle.
