About the author

I didn't have many extraordinary advantages growing up, but what I did have was the encouragement of those older than me saying, "do it while you're young." One day, when I was 17, that advice stuck and I started traveling the world. I haven't stopped and now half a lifetime later, I want to tell the world what I've learned and how YOU can live an adventurous and prosperous life!
